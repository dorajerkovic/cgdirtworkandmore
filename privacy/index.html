Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how CG Dirt Work and More ("CG Dirt Work," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit cgdirtworkandmore.com or get in touch with us. We keep this simple: we only collect what we need to answer your questions and do the work, and we do not sell your information.

By using this website or contacting us, you agree to the practices described here.

Who We Are

CG Dirt Work and More is an excavation and land services business based near Skiatook, Oklahoma. You can reach us by phone or text at (573) 280-7023 or by email at cgdirtwork@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

Information you give us. When you fill out our contact form or reach out by phone, text, or email, you may share your name, email address, phone number, the location of your property, and details about the work you need. You choose what to share, and we use it to respond to you and to provide our services.

Information collected automatically. When you visit the site, certain information is collected automatically through your browser and the tools described below. This can include your IP address, device and browser type, the pages you view, how you arrived at the site, and how long you stay. This information is general and is used to understand how the site is used, not to identify you personally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and provide an estimate or service
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • Keep the site secure and working properly
  • Meet any legal or recordkeeping obligations

Analytics and Tracking Tools

We use the following tools to understand site traffic and reach the right audience. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies and may collect information about your activity on the site.

Google Analytics. We use Google Analytics to see how visitors find and use the site, such as which pages get the most traffic and where visitors come from. Google may process this information on our behalf. You can learn more about how Google handles data at policies.google.com/privacy, and you can opt out of Google Analytics using Google's browser add-on.

Meta (Facebook) Pixel. We use the Meta Pixel to measure the results of our Facebook activity and to understand how visitors interact with the site after seeing or clicking our content. This may allow us to show relevant ads to people who have visited the site. You can manage your ad preferences through your Facebook account settings and at facebook.com/about/privacy.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help websites work and help tools like the ones above function. You can set your browser to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Some parts of the site may not work as well if you turn cookies off.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited ways:

  • Service providers and tools. The analytics and advertising tools described above, and the platform that hosts our website, process information on our behalf so the site can function.
  • Legal reasons. We may disclose information if required by law, a court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

We do not share your contact details with unrelated third parties for their own marketing.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information safe. No website or method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so while we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep the information you share for as long as needed to respond to you, complete your project, and meet our recordkeeping and legal obligations. After that, we remove or anonymize it.

Your Choices

You have control over your information:

  • You can choose not to submit the contact form and reach us by phone or text instead
  • You can ask us what information we have about you, or ask us to correct or delete it, by contacting us at the number or email below
  • You can opt out of analytics and advertising tracking using the browser settings and opt-out tools described above

Children's Privacy

This website is meant for adults and for property owners seeking our services. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will remove it.

Third-Party Links

Our site may link to other sites we do not control, such as our Google Business Profile or Facebook page. This Privacy Policy does not cover those sites, and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
