Got Questions Before You Reach Out

Answers to the questions we hear most.

Do you offer free estimates in northeast Oklahoma?

Yes. We come out, look at your property, and give you a clear estimate with no obligation. Whether it is a pond, a building pad, a driveway, or land that needs clearing across Skiatook, Owasso, or the surrounding communities, the look and the estimate are free.

How soon will I hear back after I reach out?

We get back to you quickly, usually within a day. If you need an answer faster than that, call or text (573) 280-7023 and you will reach us direct, not a call center.

What do you need from me to give an estimate?

Tell us where your property sits and what the job is, whether that is a pond that will not hold, ground that needs grading, or a drive that keeps washing out. Photos help but are not required. We do the real assessment on site.

Do you charge to come look at the property?

No. We come out to your land across Osage, Tulsa, or Rogers County, walk the job with you, and talk through what it needs at no charge.

How far out are you scheduled?

It depends on the season and what we have going. When we talk, you will get an honest timeline you can actually plan around, not a guess to get you off the phone.
