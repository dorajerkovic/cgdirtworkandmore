Your Partners for Excavation and Land Work Across Northeast Oklahoma

CG Dirt Work and More serves landowners and homeowners throughout Pawnee, Osage, Tulsa, and Rogers County and the communities around Tulsa. We take on excavation and land work within about an hour of our Tulsa-area home base, on everything from residential lots to rural acreage.

Primary Service Areas

Cleveland

Just up the road and the closest work we do. Property here runs from town lots to rural acreage along the Arkansas River, and that mix means everything from building pads to ponds and land clearing on the kind of ground this part of Oklahoma is made of.

Hominy

Hominy runs rural and agricultural, which means ponds, drainage, land clearing, and the kind of dirt work that keeps working ground usable. We do the land work that property out this way actually needs.

Sand Springs

Sand Springs sits on the west side of the Tulsa metro, where residential growth and rural acreage meet. We handle site prep, driveways, drainage, and the ground work that gets new and existing property ready across the area.

Sapulpa

Sapulpa's steady residential growth brings demand for driveways, drainage, and site prep on new and existing property. We grade and prep the ground so the work holds up season after season.

We Also Serve

We work throughout Pawnee, Osage, Tulsa, and Rogers County, not just the four primary communities. Property in Skiatook, Owasso, Collinsville, and the surrounding rural areas within our range gets the same excavation and land work.
