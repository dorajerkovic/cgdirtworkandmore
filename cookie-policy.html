What This Policy Covers

This Cookie Policy explains how CG Dirt Work and More ("CG Dirt Work," "we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on cgdirtworkandmore.com. It works alongside our Privacy Policy, which explains more about how we handle your information.

What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device when you visit. They help the site work properly, remember your preferences, and help us understand how the site is being used. Some cookies are set by us, and some are set by the tools we use, such as Google Analytics and the Meta Pixel.

Besides cookies, similar technologies like pixels and tags do related jobs. When we say "cookies" in this policy, we mean all of these.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep the website working and secure
  • Understand how visitors find and use the site so we can improve it
  • Measure the results of our Facebook activity and show relevant content to people who have visited the site

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential cookies. These keep the site running and let you move around and use its features. The site cannot work properly without them, so they are always on.

Analytics cookies. We use Google Analytics to see which pages get the most traffic, where visitors come from, and how the site is used overall. This helps us make the site more useful. These cookies are not required for the site to work.

Advertising cookies. We use the Meta (Facebook) Pixel to measure how our Facebook activity performs and to show relevant ads to people who have visited the site. These cookies are not required for the site to work.

Managing Your Cookies

You have a few ways to control cookies:

  • On our site. When you first visit, you can choose whether to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change that choice at any time through the cookie settings link in the site footer.
  • In your browser. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings. Turning off cookies may affect how parts of the site work.
  • Through the tools directly. You can opt out of Google Analytics using Google's browser add-on, and you can manage ad preferences through your Facebook account settings.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by the third-party tools we use rather than by us directly. We do not control how those companies use the information they collect. You can read more in their own privacy policies:

  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta (Facebook): facebook.com/about/privacy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. Your continued use of the site after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
