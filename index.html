About CG Dirt Work

The Job Is Done When the Ground Is Right

CG Dirt Work and More is run by Coty Goodwin, and "run by" means he is the one in the seat moving your dirt. There is no crew of strangers on your land and no foreman to track down for a straight answer. The man you call is the man on the machine, start to finish, and he is the one who stands behind the work when it is done.

We know what the dirt is going to do once the machine pulls off your property. Which way the water will find, how the soil packs down over a wet winter, what a creek bank or a graded slope wants to do come next season.

Plenty of operators can push dirt into a shape. Far fewer can tell you whether that shape holds up over time.

Common Questions About Our Dirt Work

Straight answers to what people ask us most before getting started on a project across northeast Oklahoma.

Do you offer free estimates in northeast Oklahoma?

Yes. We come out, walk the property with you, and give you a clear estimate at no charge. Whether it is a pond, a building pad, a gravel driveway, a retaining wall, or land that needs clearing across Skiatook, Owasso, Collinsville, or the surrounding communities, the look and the estimate are free.

What kind of dirt work do you do?

We handle excavation and site preparation, pond digging and excavation, retaining walls, gravel driveways, trenching and utility work, and land management and habitat work. From grading a pad for a new build to digging a stock pond that holds water through the summer, we take on the dirt work that makes property across Osage, Tulsa, and Rogers County usable.

What areas do you serve around Skiatook?

We are based near Skiatook and work throughout Osage, Tulsa, and Rogers County. That covers Skiatook, Owasso, Collinsville, Sperry, Claremore, Sand Springs, Catoosa, Hominy, and the rural communities within about 45 minutes of home base. If you are not sure whether your property falls inside our range, give us a call and we will let you know.

Neither. The owner runs CG Dirt Work and More and operates the equipment himself on every job. You work directly with the person doing the work, from the first call through the follow-up after the dirt is moved.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. CG Dirt Work and More is licensed and insured for every job we take on across northeast Oklahoma.

It depends on the season and what we have going at the time. When we talk, you will get an honest timeline you can plan around, not a guess to get you off the phone. If you need to move fast, call or text and we will tell you what is realistic.
